Odd Guys are the main sentient species on Glycnor. They are a glucose based life form and are very stretchy and can mold into almost anything. They come from a large tree deep within the kingdom of Glycnor, this tree is the main source of life forms. 

## birth and formation

Odd Guys develop within a drip from a source of glucose. Their lower body develops first before their head. Their head's sometimes develop differently based off of how long they take to drop from the droplet. Other odd guys have different size heads due to this phenomenon, whether it'll be an elongated head or a rather short head. After an odd guy is created, they are escorted to a cold environment or nursery. From there they will harden to a gelatin and their colors will form. Some say their colors are based off of their personality but no one truly knows what they mean.
